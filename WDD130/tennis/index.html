Believe.

Here at PlayITForward we believe in coaching for everyone. Coaches help you to improve no matter your skill level, even professionals. Serena Williams, one of the best tennis players in the world, has a coach. Coaches make the difference. A coach is someone who is watching from the outside, who can analyze your technique, footwork and stroke and make recommendations based on their knowledge and expertise. A personal coach can be the difference for you, between your best casual game with friends, or becoming the best player at your school. It’s your time and your money and you want to spend it wisely. So, we welcome you to Play it forward where you’ll find what you’re looking for in coaching and progress.

Meet the coaches

Colin Bapta has been playing tennis since he was 8 and is a USPTA coach and former Div I player. He has been teaching players of all ages for the past 11 years in the Northern Virginia region. He is passionate about the game, passionate about his students and dedicated to the improvement of all students—from beginner to advanced. He holds a bachelors in Sports and Physical Education from George Mason University. He played tennis throughout his college years and traveled all over the world to compete in tournaments against some of the best players in the world. Colin communicates with elementary and middle school children with ease but can also improve the game for adults, casual and serious players.

Steve Tignor is a dedicated player and coach just reaching his stride in the field of coaching. He is studying for his USPTA certification and has been coaching for 8 years. He’s a powerhouse player and has a bachelors in Health and Physical Education from Old Dominion University. He coached throughout his college years and hasn’t stopped in the 4 years since graduating and moving to the northern Virginia area. His students rave about his energy and drive, we know we’ll be seeing a lot from him in the future.
